### 🚀 Mission Summary
I am a **Machine Learning Engineer** and **IEEE-published researcher** currently pursuing my **MS in Software Engineering at Arizona State University**. My work focuses on developing mission-critical AI systems for aerospace and industrial applications, with a heavy emphasis on **Security-Focused MLOps** and **Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)**.

As a **Honeywell Aerospace Innovation Fellow** at the ASU Technology Innovation Lab, I specialize in flight optimization and autonomous systems.
